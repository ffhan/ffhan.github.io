Merlin

This is a copy of a "Simon" game. The goal is to remember in which order the squares are lit. The game begins by lighting 4 random squares, and then asks you to light the squares in the correct order. You win a level when you give the exact order,and each increasing level shows an additional square. [Source]

Controls

  • 4 - left up
  • 5 - right up
  • 7 - left down
  • 8 - right down
